About

Bridging the gap between emerging technology and real classrooms.

I'm Ryan Seymour. I spent twenty years in education, first as an ESL and science teacher, then building the technology meant to help teachers do their jobs. Along the way I shipped AI tools at Amplify, the mobile release of Minecraft Education at Mojang, and science curriculum at the Smithsonian. I'm based in Arlington, Virginia.

I started Seymour Learning Insights because I kept seeing the same gap: AI gets pushed to schools and EdTech teams, but there is not a lot of guidance on how to use these tools effectively while retaining agency. I work in between, helping teams and educators put AI to use in ways that actually fit how they already work.
