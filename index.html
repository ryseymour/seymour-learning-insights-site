The thesis — “Beyond Gamification”

AI in education is about to repeat the gamification mistake.

“Gamify it” became points and badges that raised anxiety. “AI it” is heading the same way.

A decade ago, the pressure was to bolt game mechanics onto learning — and we got leaderboards that undermined the very motivation they promised. Today the pressure is to bolt AI onto everything. The fix is the same: design from how people actually learn — autonomy, competence, and connection — and treat the technology as a tool in service of a teacher's judgment, not a replacement for it.
